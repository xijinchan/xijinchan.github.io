Audiofixup Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Audiofixup does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The extension runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any servers. No analytics, tracking, or third-party services are used. All settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome’s storage API.

The extension may require certain permissions (e.g., "storage", "activeTab", etc.) solely to provide its core functionality. These permissions are not used to access or collect personal information.
